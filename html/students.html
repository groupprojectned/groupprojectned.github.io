Students




An Unlocked.me Student account is perfect for fulltime students between the ages of 16 and 26 . You get a host of free transactions plus monthly lifestyle benefits . It's your life, go on, UNLOCK it. Unlock your lifestyle, your potential and your money with an Unlocked.me Student account.

Features and benefits

For a fixed monthly fee of just R20, you get:

  • Free debit card phurchases
  • Free withdrawals at Nedbank ATMs
  • Free debit and stop orders
  • Free balance enquires at Nedbank ATMs and self service channels
  • Free statement enquires at Nedbank ATMs and internet banking

Who qualifies

  • Full time student aged 16-26 years old
  • Non-residents are welcome to apply, at an additional fee of R35 pere month
  • Not available to trusts, companies or legal entities

Lifestyle benefits

Unlock great lifestyle benefits with your Unlocked.me Student account. Choose any 2 of these 4 benefits monthly. Your benefits are guaranteed as long as you make one deposit and transact three times each month.

  • 100 MB data bundle
  • R30 online Debonairs voucher
  • 2 for 1 Nu Metro tickets
  • R150 TravelStart voucher

To redeem dial *120*4545#.

What you need to apply?

  • your valid South African identity document or smart identiy card or A Passport with a valid study permit.
  • No proof of residence required
  • Proof of registration at a tertiary institution: confirmston letterhead of trhe tertiary institution at which you are registered; or a valid sstudent card; or a letter of acceptance by the tertiary institution, on an official letterhead.

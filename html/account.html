Account



A no-frills transparent account with a flat monthly fee, allowing you to pay only for what you use and nothing more. You can add additional benefits when required.

Features and benefits

  • Free card swipes
  • Free balance enquiries at self-assisted channels (Nedbank ATM, SMS banking, online banking, Money app)
  • Free email statements
  • Free stop orders

Who qualifies?

  • Individuals over the age of 16 years.
  • Non-residents are welcome to apply, at an additional monthly fee of R35.
  • Not available to trusts, companies or legal entities.

What you need to apply?

  • Your valid South African identity card/document or passport with valid permit.
  • Proof of residence, such as a utility bill, rental invoice or a phone account that has your name and physical address on it.

Optional extras

  • eNote SMS notifications
  • Airtime topup at Nedbank ATMs and self-service channels (SMS banking, online banking, Money app)
  • Send money to any cellphone in South Africa with Send-iMali
  • Buy prepaid electricity

Use your cellphone to pay In-store, Online, Anywhere.

Now you can pay with your card at any QR code using the Nedbank Money app scan to pay.

Convinience

  • No need for multiple apps on your phone, the Nedbank Money app allows you to scan to pay majority of QR codes in South Africa (Masterpass, SnapScan and Zapper).
  • No need to load and maintain cards in app for scan to pay.
  • You can even block, replace and scan to pay before you get your replacement plastic.
  • Security - Authorise every scan to pay purchase with Money App login details.
  • Earn rewards - Earn Greenbacks and Membership Rewards points at same rate as card purchases.
  • Zero rated app - Do not use a lot of data as the Nedbank Money app is a zero rated app.

Seniors



A full service transactional account if you are 55 years and over. Maintain a R10 000 investment account and you can enjoy significant fee reductions on your transactional account plus the convenience of free digital banking anytime, anywhere.

Overview

Get the most out of your day-to-day banking by maintaining a balance of at least R10 000 in any Nedbank fixed deposit, notice deposit or JustSave Account. This account, and many of your transactions are free, as well as free digital banking and cheque book.

Features and benefits

By maintaining a minimum balance of R10 000 in any Nedbank fixed deposit, notice deposit or JustSave Account you get

  • Free monthly fee
  • Unlimited cheque deposits at Nedbank branches and ATMs
  • Four Nedbank ATM cash withdrawals. Two free over-the-counter cash withdrawals
  • Cheque payments
  • Unlimited debit and stop orders
  • A debit card
  • Unlimited interaccount transfers and third-party payments through Nedbank self-service banking channels (cellphone, telephone and internet banking)
  • First in-branch balance enquiry of every month. Balance enquiries at Nedbank ATMs and through other self-service banking channels
  • First statement enquiry of every month at Nedbank branches
  • Convenience of free digital banking anytime, anywhere (online banking, Nedbank Money App and SMS banking)
  • A Gold Cheque card
  • A Garage Card
  • An overdraft facility*
  • Membership to Nedbank Greenbacks Programme

The account comes with the following optional extras:

Who qualifies?

  • Individuals aged 55 years or over
  • Non-residents are welcome to apply, at an additional monthly fee of R35
  • Not available to trusts, companies or legal entities

What you need to apply?

  • Your valid barcoded identity document, smart identity card or passport with a valid permit.
  • Proof of residence, such as a utility bill, rental invoice or a phone account that has your name and physical address on it.

Additional benefits

  • Free will drafting**
  • Free financial planning session
